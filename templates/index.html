This website draws weather data from the Ecowitt family of sensors (pictured here) and the current weather condition from WeatherAPI.com. Data is extracted by WeeWX and held in a MySQL Database.

The site utilises Random Forest AI calculations to make local weather predictions about Temperature and Conditions, as well as the chance of Rain and Lightning in the next 24-48 hours.
